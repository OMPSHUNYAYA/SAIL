Risk formula:

risk score = severity weight + condition weight

Severity weights:

LOW = 1
MEDIUM = 2
HIGH = 3
CRITICAL = 4

Condition weights:

NORMAL = 0
TIMEOUT = 2
DEGRADED = 2
CRITICAL_FAILURE = 4

Triage boundaries:

score < 3 -> LOW
score >= 3 -> MEDIUM
score >= 5 -> HIGH
score >= 7 -> CRITICAL
